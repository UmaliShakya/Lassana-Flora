About Us

Lassana Flora was founded by Dr. Lasantha Malavige (MBBS, DIPM, Ph.D.) while he was a third-year medical student at the Medical Faculty, Colombo in 1998. Since then, Lassana Flora has revolutionized Sri Lankaâ€™s floral industry by rendering professional services and carefully developed quality assured procedures for producing the best floral arrangements. These efforts have not only helped Lassana Flora to create floral solutions at very competitive prices but also build its reputation as the foremost florist in the country with cost-effective and innovative floral designs.

Lassana Flora has a state-of-the-art workshop and cool room facility at its Homagama stores complex and operates via five branches located at Nawala, Colombo 07, Colombo City Center, Kandy City Center, Negombo Panadura and Embilipitiya.

Its biggest strength is the well experienced and professional staff and over a decade of experience in the industry which has made it the market leader in supplying wedding flowers and floral solutions for corporate events. The constant introduction of innovative designs and different floral solutions has made Lassana Flora the floral solutions provider of choice for many leading international events in Sri Lanka.

Lassana Flora specializes in Poruwa, Church, Hindu and Muslim weddings and enjoys the reputation as being the only wedding florist in Sri Lanka to use a fleet of freezer trucks to deliver flowers that maintain freshness.

Lassana Flora also offers it clientele the option of ordering flower arrangements via its hotline (011 200 11 22) as well as its website www.lassanaflora.com.. This website provides a link to all the services offered as well as an extensive range of online flower selections that millions of people across the world could order. Our online wedding planning service has helped many foreign nationals and Sri Lankan expatriates to have their weddings in Sri Lanka.
