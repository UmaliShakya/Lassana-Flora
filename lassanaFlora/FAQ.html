F A Q

Q: How are orders placed?

Answer

Can be done via the happiness link or calling the happiness hotline +94 (0)112 00 11 22.

Q: How are the flowers delivered?

Answer

Delivery is made in our own vehicles equipped with freezers to the desired destination.

Q: What happens if my recipient is not present at the time of delivery?

Answer

We will do our best to call recipient or sender and deliver to a nearby location.

Q: Could I have same day delivery of gift bouquets?

Answer

Only available for orders placed before 10 am the same day. Click here for same day delivery selection.

Q: Can I place an order for international delivery?

Answer

Please call us our happiness hotline +94 (0)112 00 11 22

Q: Do you offer free delivery?

Answer

Only for orders above LKR 1850 within Colombo and specified suburbs. Click to check free delivery areas.

Q: Do you deliver on weekends/ public holidays?

Answer

We are only closed for the Sinhala Tamil New Year and May Day.

Q: Do you provide round the clock service?

Answer

Yes – but midnight deliveries are done for a special charge.

Q: How do I check the status of my order?

Answer

The customer is informed after delivery via an e-mail and please click for order status.

Q: Can my order be a surprise?

Answer

Of course, but the address and directions with the correct landmarks must be clearly stated.

Q: What if the city I want is not listed?

Answer

Please call our happiness hotline for special delivery arrangements.

Q: Is it safe to shop at lassanaflora.com?

Answer

Yes, its secure but basic precautions on web browsing should be followed.

Q: What are the modes of payment?

Answer

Visa, Master, American Express Cards, Cash On Delivery (Selected range), Bank Transfer and cheques are accepted from corporate customers. Island wide Delivery Same Day Delivery & Express Delivery are available for selected products & regions .
